Gas Reset

If the hot water stops

  1. Please check that the water heater remote control is turned on.
  2. If there is still no hot water anywhere in the room, the gas safety meter may have stopped the gas.
  3. Please press and hold the reset button on the gas meter outside.
  4. Wait for a few minutes, then try the hot water again.
