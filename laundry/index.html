Laundry

💧 Detergent, fabric softener, and laundry nets are stored near the washing machine — feel free to use them!

How to use the washing machine

  • ① Press ON/OFF to turn on
  • ② Select mode: Wash→Dry / Wash only / Dry only
  • ③ Select course: Regular or Quick
  • ④ Put detergent in the RIGHT slot, softener in the LEFT slot
  • ⑤ Press START/STOP to begin
